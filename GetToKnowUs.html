Our Team

Meet the STAR Reader team! We are a dynamic team of four University of Cape Town students, each bringing unique expertise to our mission of transforming education. STAR stands for Schae, Toby, Aaliyah and Rushnie. With Schae in the business analyst role, she ensures that we align our goals with strategic insights and come up with the best possible solution. Toby, our soft skills, fosters effective communication, problem-solving and collaboration within the team. As the project manager, Aaliyah orchestrates our efforts, keeping us organized, motivated and focused. Rushnie, our software developer, brings our ideas to life through her technical expertise. Sponsored by Enterprise Solutions 365, we are driven by a shared passion to collaborate with multiple schools and educational institutions to make a meaningful impact in the educational world.

Get to know our sponsor

Enterprise Solutions 365 (ES365), founded in 2016 by Adiel Kader and Uthmaan Hendricks, emerged from more than 25 years of experience in various industry sectors including oil, finance, retail and government. They have delivered enterprise grade technology solutions to organisations in more than 20 countries in Africa and Europe. ES365 assists businesses is assessing their strategic and operational challenges to propose real-world technology solutions (from ideation to implementation), guiding business to change the way they think to obtain better business value for the organisation and better products and services for their customers. ES365 aims to span the gap between the aspirational objectives of an organisation and the delivery of its technology-based solutions that add real business value. ES365 has assisted the Western Cape Education Department (WCED) in technology strategy, solutioning, problem resolution, optimisation, migration and security improvements of their most significant education systems. ES365 is currently assisting WCED in reviewing their use of existing technology platforms in terms of their long- term citizen value delivery objectives.
